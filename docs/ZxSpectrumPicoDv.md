### ZxSpectrumPicoDv
This target matches the [Pimoroni Pico DV](https://shop.pimoroni.com/products/pimoroni-pico-dv-demo-base) board:

<img src="P1040672_1500x1500.png" width="300"/>

It supports the following:
* USB keyboard
* USB joysticks
* 50/60Hz HDMI video
* HDMI/I2S audio
* SPI SD card
* Ringo's low resolution mode
